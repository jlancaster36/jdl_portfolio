I grew up in Woodstock, Georgia attending high school in Roswell at Blessed Trinity. As a budding software engineer, I blend a passion for innovative technologies with a strong foundation in computer science. My journey at Georgia Tech, pursuing a Bachelor's and Master's in Computer Science with concentrations in Artificial Intelligence, Media, and Computer Graphics, reflects my dedication to the field. I have honed my skills in a range of programming languages and platforms, from Java to Python, and AWS to Snowflake.

With nearly two years of internship experience between Xultrix LLC and Kudelski Security, I've had some excellent opporunities to hone and showcase my various software skills in a professional environment.

Outside work, I have a wide range of personal interests including writing, video editing, and kick boxing. A fun fact is that on top of my major in computer science at Georgia Tech, I have a minor in Science Fiction Studies on account of my love for storytelling and speculative fiction. I write long fiction, short fiction, screenplays, and poetry, and once a month you can catch me at Georgia Tech's Open Mic Night testing my latest pieces. I also ran a youtube channel for a competetive Overwatch group called R9 where I editted a montages and made several graphics.

That more or less covers the highlights. Feel free to reach out on my socials below!
