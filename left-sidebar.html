Orbit

Orbit is a passion project being developed by myself and a few friends in Unity. The blueprint is a character driven 2D top down space-sim about small people finding meaning in a vast and sometimes indifferent seeming universe.

The gameplay will focus on assembling a ship crew that the player will come to develop unique and rich relationships with. These relationships will manifest in the crew's chemistry when met with ship to ship combat.

Explore new worlds, make new friends, and forge meaning from the lonliness of Orbit.
